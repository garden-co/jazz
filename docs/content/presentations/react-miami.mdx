# Modern real-time databases and sync engines

- Zero
- ElectricSQL
- InstantDB
- Convex
- Turso
- Jazz

<Notes>
Turns out other people had similar ideas and also really wanted
to simplify the stack.

This is the now well-established category of real-time databases
and sync engines.

I'll first describe what these new roughly have in common - because
the most important idea is what this general new stack looks like.

After that we can dive into some unique Jazz things, but for now
the main message is: you deserve a better stack.

</Notes>
</Slide>

<Slide>
# Metrics to judge a stack by

- Lines of code
- Layers of infra BS
- Light-milliseconds

<Notes>
And the three metrics I want you to keep in mind when
you judge a stack are

Lines of code, especially the ones that don't have anything to
do with what makes your app unique.

Layers of infra bullshit, meaning how many things you have to
deploy and integrate with each other.

Light-milliseconds that user edits have to travel before they
become visible.

</Notes>
</Slide>

  - Database
