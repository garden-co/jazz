Adds React Native (Expo) as a framework option in the interactive scaffolder, alongside Next.js and SvelteKit. The new option scaffolds one of three starters — `expo-localfirst`, `expo-hybrid`, or `expo-betterauth` — depending on the chosen auth mode. Wires `EXPO_PUBLIC_JAZZ_*` env keys for cloud-hosted setup.
